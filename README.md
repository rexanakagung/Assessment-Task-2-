# Assessment-Task-2

## PART A: Data Selection and Attributes.
I have chose these attributes as they are the main factors of a car, they are easily interpereted and flexible. This allows  players to have the ability to understand these attributes as they are extremely simple, these are the Acceleration, engine size, Braking Speed, Horsepower, Year and Top speed of a car.
